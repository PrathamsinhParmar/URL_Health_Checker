Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026 — Version 1.2

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This transparency is key to how we maintain UptimeChecker as a reliable platform.

  • Account Information: We store your username, email address, and encrypted password credentials provided during registration.
  • Monitoring Data: We store metadata about the URLs you add, including check history, response times, and status changes.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect data on how often you log in and which features you interact with to improve the service quality.

2. How We Use Information

The primary purpose of collecting data is to operate and improve the Monitoring Service we provide to you.

  • To maintain the core heartbeat of the service — automatically checking your URLs.
  • To trigger alerts via your preferred notification channels when downtime is detected.
  • To provide technical support and troubleshoot issues specific to your profile.

3. Data Security & Integrity

UptimeChecker utilizes industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) for data in transit and robust hashing algorithms for sensitive data at rest. We never share your private monitoring data with third parties.

4. Contact Our Team

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this policy, please don't hesitate to reach out to our privacy team at privacy@uptimechecker.com.
